Our Programs

The Web Development Academy

Overview

The Web Development Academy is designed to give adults with little to no development experience a flexible and affordable way of gaining the skills necessary to become developers. The Academy is both selective and rigorous. We don’t measure success by graduation rates - we measure success by the quality of tech professionals we produce. To ensure that we are producing the best graduates, we focus on hard technical skills as well as soft professional skills.

Application and Program Schedule

We take applications on a rolling basis. This means our students aren’t tied to semesters or sessions and have the flexibility they need.

Although the foundation of the program is designed to be completed in 12 weeks, students are able to move at their own pace, taking as little or as much time to complete the program as needed.

Application and Acceptance Process

  1. You complete your program application.

  2. We review your application and you receive an acceptance decision within 7 days.

  3. If you are not accepted we encourage you to reapply at a later date.

  4. If you are accepted, you have 7 days to confirm your start date and make your first payment.

  5. You are ready to start!


Coder Labs

Coder Labs is the Coded by U dev shop. Coder Labs takes in real projects from non-profit organizations and mission-driven small businesses. Our students form the development teams for Coder Labs projects, with guidance and supervision from our instructors. Students who work on Coder Labs projects are paid, which gives them the ability to earn income while gaining real world experience.


Coded by U Fellows

Coded by U is committed to providing adults who are economically disadvantaged or underrepresented in tech with the skills necessary to gain access to opportunities in the tech industry. Through the Coded by U Fellows program, we are able to maintain this commitment by providing talented adults with scholarships that cover the cost of their educations.


Intro to Startups

We want to produce both great employees and employers. To support this effort, we also work to help people gain a solid foundation in startup focused entrepreneurship. Through our Intro to Startups we help students gain a fundamental understanding of how startups work. This program starts by teaching students the difference between a startup and small business, then walks students through each aspect of startup.

WHAT IS CODED BY U


Overview

Initially, Coded by U was created as a Coded by Kids program designed to provide low-to-middle income adults with affordable and flexible coding-focused education classes that prepare them for entry-level development careers. Now a separate company, Coded by U maintains both an operating relationship and a financial commitment to Coded by Kids through resource sharing and program partnerships. Additionally, profits from Coded by U are used to fund free Coded by Kids programs for inner city children.


Our Mission

Coded by U works to provide diverse groups of adults with affordable and flexible, but highly rigorous project based programming and design focused education programs.

Dev Work for Deserving Non-profits

Through Coder Labs, we are giving people the abilty to gain real-world experience working on projects for great non-profits and mission driven startups.
