About Us

We Grew Out Of A Commitment To Social Change

Coded by U is the adult education focused member of the Coded by Kids community. Like Coded by Kids, Coded by U was born out of a commitment to providing programming courses that are accessible to everyone.

Through our commitment, we provide pathways into tech for adults who either belong to an underrepresented group in tech or are unable to afford a traditional coding education program.

Our Commitment To Coded by Kids

Along with our commitment to creating pathways into tech for adults Coded by U has a direct commitment to Coded by Kids. The profits from student tuition from Coded by U classes are used to support the free classes that Coded by Kids provides to disadvantaged children.

HOW CODED BY U WORKS


Rigorous Coding Focused Education Programs for Adults

Coded by U provides rigorous project based coding education programs. We offer fundamental programs designed for people with little to no coding experience along with advanced programs for experienced developers.

Offered at convenient times and in easily accessible locations our programs are setup to give working adults the ability to gain new tech skills and enhance current skills.


Plug and Play Tech Focused Education Programs

Coded by U has developed tech focused education programs that can be plugged into existing businesses and organizations. By providing organizations with high quality, managed tech education solutions we give our partners the ability to leverage our expertise to add tech education to their offerings. This enables organizations like coworking spaces and schools to add greater value to what they offer without the cost of staffing and managing a new program.


Workforce Development

As the labor needs of industry and our economy change, how we train people to go out into the workforce and the jobs we train people for also need to change. We help organizations that focus on workforce development to provide coding focused workforce development programs to the people they serve.

Nontraditional Pipelines to Diverse Tech Talent

Coded by U Fellows

If you are interested in attending the Coded by U Web Development Academy, but need financial assistance or you belong to a group that is currently underrepresented in tech we would like to encourage you to apply to become a Coded by U fellow.

The Coded by U Fellows programs is highly selective, but provides fellows with a scholarship to attend our Web Development Academy along with professional development and networking opportunities

If you are interested in the Coded by U Fellows Program, please contact us.

Dev Work for Deserving Non-profits

Through our development shop (Coding Good), we are giving people the abilty to gain real-world experience working on projects for great non-profits and socially-focused startups. While providing people with real world experience, Coding Good provides non-profits and socially focused startups with high-quality, affordable development work. Coding Good's mission is to provide organizations that do great work with the development work they need accomplish their missions.

If you know a non-profit or social startup that could benefit from our help or you would like join Coding Good, please contact us.
