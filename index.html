About Us

We Grew Out Of A Commitment To Social Change

Coded by U is the adult education focused member of the Coded by Kids community. Like Coded by Kids, Coded by U was born out of a commitment to providing programming courses that are accessible to every one.

Through our commitment, we provide low-income adults with low-cost web application development education opportunities, along with pathways into web development careers.

HOW CODED BY U WORKS

There are four key components to Coded by U:
1. The Web Development Fundamentals Program
2. Our Community Courses
3. The Apprenticeship Program
4. The Coding Good Dev Shop

For every Coded by U Community Course that someone pays for, a deserving member of the Coded by U community will gain free or highly reduced cost access to our Web Development Fundamentals Program.

After completing the Coded by U Web Development Fundamentals Course, participants are offered to either enter a paid apprenticeship with one of our partner companies or work as an apprentice in our development shop (The Coding Good Dev Shop).

HOW TO GET INVOLVED

Are you interested in joining our community by taking one of our classes? Do you want to teach a class, host or sponsor a class, or just want to show your support? Check out the information below!


YOU'RE A DEVELOPER OR DESIGNER

We are always looking for great developers and designers who want to help us accomplish our mission by teaching one of our classes. If you are passionate about design and development and want to use this passion to help others, please submit your information via our instructor information form.

YOU WANT TO TAKE A CLASS

Have you always wanted an opportunity to get into the tech industry, but couldn't afford a traditional computer science education program? Our programs are your pathway to that opportunity. Are you an experienced developer or designer looking for a course to enhance your skills? We have programs for you as well. Check out our list of upcoming classes.

YOU'RE A COMPANY OR COWORKING SPACE

Do you represent a company that would like to partner with us on curriculum design for our classes? Is your company interested in our apprenticeship program? Would your company like to sponsor one of our programs? Do you represent a coworking space that is interested in partnering with us to offer classes? Please send us your information, so we can get in touch or read more about partnership opportunities.

YOU WANT TO BE A SUPPORTER OR VOLUNTEER

While our volunteer instructors are critical to our mission, we also need volunteers that play an important role outside of the classroom. If you want to volunteer with us in a non-teaching role, please check out some of our open volunteer positions and get in touch with us.

Dev Work for Deserving Non-profits

Through our development shop (Coding Good), we are giving people the abilty to gain real-world experience working on projects for great non-profits and socially-focused startups. While providing people with real world experience, Coding Good provides non-profits and socially focused startups with high-quality, affordable development work. Coding Good's mission is to provide organizations that do great work with the development work they need accomplish their missions.

If you know a non-profit or social startup that could benefit from our help or you would like join Coding Good, please contact us.
